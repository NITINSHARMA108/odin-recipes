Chopped Brussels Sprout Salad

Description

I just woke up to a snowy white Saturday morning, which means that it’s the perfect day for… Brussels sprout salad! Ok, maybe I’m the weird one that just loves salad no matter what’s going on around me.

If you’re craving something warm and cozy, go make a mug of this cacao hot chocolate and then come back here if you’re looking for an easy Brussels sprout salad to make for weekend holiday festivities. This one will be your go-to throw-together winter salad that you’ll crave when you want a break from cookies and other indulgent treats

Ingredients

  • 1 shallot, minced
  • ¼ cup sunflower seed oil
  • ¼ cup cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • ½ teaspoon honey
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 cups Brussels sprouts, trimmed, halved, and sliced
  • ½ cup dried cranberries
